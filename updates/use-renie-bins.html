Use the Renie.io bins

What is a Renie.io bin?

Using the renie bins will save the planet and give people a free raffle ticket for anything they want. Renie.io bins are trusted by the Dubai Muncipality and converts the recieved plastic to clothes for construction workers. This is a very smart way of encourage people to recycle.

How do I use it?

Just create an account on the Renie app, click on the scan qr button, go to a bin, scan the qr and drop bottles one by one. For every bottle, you get a raffle ticket.

Michael Silfani

Michael Silfani has taken two big bags and dropped them all in the renie bin. He had recived many tickets to drop on the website. Big shoutout and thanks to Michael!
